Welcome to Good Day Kampot

A Warm, Family-Run Guesthouse in the Vibrant Town of Kampot, Cambodia.

Since August 2025, Keo and his wife have proudly owned and managed the guesthouse, bringing their deep local roots and passion for genuine Cambodian hospitality to every guest’s experience. As a true family business, Good Day Kampot offers more than just accommodation—it provides a welcoming home base where travelers can feel supported, inspired, and connected to the local community.

With extensive experience in business management and hospitality, Keo and his wife personally oversee daily operations, ensuring that every detail—from comfortable rooms to personalized local recommendations—is thoughtfully handled. Their hands-on approach guarantees authentic service and seamless stays, making Good Day Kampot a standout choice for travelers seeking a family-friendly, locally owned guesthouse in Kampot.

Supporting this family-driven vision, Andrew leads the digital marketing and technology initiatives, leveraging advanced online strategies to enhance the guesthouse’s visibility and continuously improve the guest experience. This combination of local expertise and innovative marketing ensures Good Day Kampot remains a top-rated guesthouse in Kampot, known for its authentic atmosphere, exceptional value, and personalized service.

Guests at Good Day Kampot enjoy not only comfortable accommodations but also access to valuable services such as motorbike rentals, travel agency support, and visa assistance—making it the ideal home base for exploring Kampot’s natural beauty and cultural attractions.

Discover the genuine warmth and authentic charm of a family-run guesthouse in Kampot. We invite you to experience hospitality that feels like home, with the added convenience and expertise of a dedicated local team.
